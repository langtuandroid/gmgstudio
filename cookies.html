This Cookies Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They are used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the site owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To enable certain functions of the website
  • To provide analytics and understand site usage
  • To store your preferences
  • To deliver personalized content or advertisements

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for basic site functionality.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with the website.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads and marketing campaigns.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies. Please note that some features of our site may not work properly without cookies.

5. Third-Party Cookies
